pop

why pop

In order to get to know better the general mood caused by the pandemy of COVID-19, the emotions and state of mind have been analysed. People felt bored, with non-cooperative digital services that could relieve the weight of their “social distancing” conditions and monotonous daily routines. This is the concept behind POP, an app that can fill up the empty moments, offering fun, original and offline distractions from the everyday life.

It’s Boredom o’Clock

people feel bored and rely less and less on technology for their free time activities, as it offers unattractive and repetitive options that don’t feel productive. This lead to the idea of offering original activities that could create a connection with reality.

Routine Disruptor

as the routine and everyday life is repetitive, people need a distraction, a spark of originality and change to the monotony of it, to be able to exploit the empty moments for the better and meaningfully.
