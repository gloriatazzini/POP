pop

Digital services are now helping in many areas, especially during critical moments of crisis. POP is a project born in one of these situations, thought to relieve the weight of endless days at home, characterized by repetitiveness and boredom.

And you, what would you do in your empty moments?

The answers to this survey are anonymous and not trackable. The feedbacks received will be deployed to improve the user experience, increase the number and variety of activities proposed and enhance the accuracy of suggestions.

Submit

keep yourself entertained

POP, even if through engaging experiences ad push notifications, is not the usual life coach. It is meant to relate with the users in a friendly and easy-going way, to help them distract with fun from the common empty moments.

in a unique way

This all works thanks to customized original activities, that get you involved firsthand and that provide and eccentric alternative to the daily tasks, bringing a note of uncommonness to the usual life.

away from the phone

A must of the contents suggested by POP is the idea to completely disconnect from the screens, helping the users rediscover reality by playing with it and all of its possibilities, leaving the phone for some time and take advantage of the physical opportunities around.
