how it works

In order to propose the best of user experiences, POP has its technological roots in the reality of recommender systems, which use system parameters and personal preferences to deliver a product that mirrors the user’s interests.

Screen Time

it is the time spent on the phone each day, distinguishing the apps used in categories, such as entertainment, social media, productivity, education, creativity…

Date
and time

it is a system service connected to satellites that automatically synchronizes the smartphone to the date and time of the place the user is at

Weather forecasts

a system app that is strictly connected to the GPS one, meant to provide meteorological information and updates on the location in which the user is

Steps' count

introduced not many years ago, it is an app that often comes with the Health one, made to keep track of the physical activity during the day and the steps made

GPS Location

it provides geolocation and time information, in order to monitor the physical movements of the person and also analyse the context conditions
