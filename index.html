what's pop?

POP is an app born to play with reality through experiences collected in boxes. The main technology used is the one of recommender systems, which can select the most accurate choices getting to know the user and analysing the context, making the best of each free moment available. Like this, POP’s uniqueness relies on the discovery of new original and offline activities, to transform each empty moment in a meaningful occasion.

Learn More

why

During an health emergency situation like the one caused by COVID-19, boredom has sneaked in people’s houses, rendering each day monotonous and repetitive, without any original distraction and offline activity to keep entertained.

Learn More

how

A recommender system based on content filtering is the key of our experience: each user is suggested a series of boxes and activities according to his or her personal interests and what the phone says about him or her, analysing system parameters to deliver the best for them.
